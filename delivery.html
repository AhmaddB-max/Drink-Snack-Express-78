Delivery Requirements

What you need to know before ordering

🎂

Age Verification

Valid government-issued ID required at delivery. Must be 21 years or older. We do not deliver to anyone who appears intoxicated.

📍

Delivery Address

We deliver to residential addresses only. Clear instructions and working buzzer/intercom help ensure smooth delivery.

💳

Payment

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Payment is processed securely at checkout.

📦

Signature Required

Adult signature required at delivery. If you're unavailable, we'll leave a notice and attempt redelivery or arrange pickup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our delivery service

What are your delivery hours? +
We offer delivery 7 days a week from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Express delivery is available during all operating hours, while standard delivery cutoff times may vary by location.
Do you deliver to apartments and condos? +
Yes! We deliver to apartments, condos, and multi-unit buildings. Please provide clear delivery instructions including building access codes, unit numbers, and any special directions to help our drivers find you quickly.
What if I'm not home for delivery? +
Since adult signature is required for alcohol delivery, someone 21+ must be present to receive the order. If no one is available, our driver will leave a notice and you can reschedule delivery or arrange for pickup at our location.
Can I track my order? +
Absolutely! Once your order is out for delivery, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can follow your driver's location in real-time on a map and get updates on estimated arrival time.
What's your cancellation policy? +
Orders can be cancelled free of charge up to 15 minutes after placement. Once your order has been assigned to a driver, cancellation fees may apply. Contact our customer service team immediately if you need to cancel.
Do you offer contactless delivery? +
Yes! While we still need to verify age and require a signature, we offer a contactless option where the driver verifies your ID from a safe distance and you can sign electronically on their device.
